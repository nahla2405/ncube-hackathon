About page!

Em_art is a place for you to develop your future
We connect people "in need" to people "who need". The people who have those abilities such as tutoring, photography, crocheting, painting, etc, will therefore primarily be connected to the clients who want to purchase a specific work or who want the skill work at a reduced price. The price of the product will gradually increase as there are more buyers, but this will also give anyone with a skill that isn't actively sought after a chance to shine by bringing their work to light. Additionally, this will make it simpler for clients who want to buy a skill or a piece of work but don't have a lot of money to support them with the little money they do.As a result, a relationship can be formed through mutual cooperation, in which they can help each other while meeting their own needs
